#TEAMTREES

By MrBeast and Mark Rober



MrBeast

The movement #TeamTrees was started by philanthropist YouTuber MrBeast with an aim to plant 20 million trees wherever land can be acquired.


People from all over the world were asked to donate $1 for a single tree to be planted, grown and nurtured.


Tesla and SpaceX owner Elon Musk made a contribution of a million dollars to this cause.

The movement managed to garner over 22 million dollars under 2 years and has contributed significantly in fighting climate change.
